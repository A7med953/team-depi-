Key Features & Services

Powerful tools designed for all users in the healthcare environment, streamlining workflows and enhancing patient care.

Appointment Scheduling

Efficient appointment booking and management system with real-time availability tracking and automated reminders.

Patient Records

Comprehensive electronic health records with complete medical history, test results, and treatment plans.

Doctor Dashboard

Intuitive interface for doctors to manage patients, view schedules, and update medical records efficiently.

Prescription Management

Digital prescription system with medication tracking, dosage management, and pharmacy integration.

Patient Portal

Self-service portal for patients to access records, book appointments, and communicate with healthcare providers.

Analytics & Reports

Comprehensive reporting tools with insights into hospital operations, patient outcomes, and resource utilization.
